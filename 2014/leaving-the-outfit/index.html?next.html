Leaving The Outfit

by Dan Perrera

Today I’d like to announce that I’ve left The Outfit, a company that I co-founded more than two years ago. I learned a lot, grew professionally, worked with great clients and skilled craftspeople — but now it’s time for a change. I’d like to say thank you to my former business partners for allowing me to come along for the ride.

What’s next? I’m not exactly sure, but I don’t think I’ll be able to stay idle for long. If you have an interesting problem that needs solving, don’t be afraid to get in touch.
